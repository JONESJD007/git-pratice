Singapore is a vibrant city-state located at the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula. Known for its impressive skyline and modern lifestyle, it is a melting pot of cultures, blending Chinese, Malay, and Indian influences. Major attractions include the iconic Marina Bay Sands, the lush Gardens by the Bay, and the historic Singapore Botanic Gardens. Despite its small size, Singapore boasts a high standard of living and is a global hub for finance, technology, and trade.
